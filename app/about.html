About FTOS

FTOS is a browser extension designed to save you from the tedious task of reading long and complicated Terms of Service documents.

We believe that users should have a clear understanding of the agreements they enter into online. Our tool uses AI to scan, analyze, and summarize any TOS document you encounter. It provides a concise summary and a simple ranking, highlighting any potential red flags or user-unfriendly clauses.

Our mission is to bring transparency and clarity to the web, one click at a time.
